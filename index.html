What is Inclusive AI?

As smart as AIs are today, not all are inclusive. Inclusive AI is the stadard that will push the world one step closer towards equality. It is made possible, and only possible, under the collective efforts pf AI developers to be aware of inclusiveness and to think beyond human biases, intentionally or unintentionally, while developing AI applications. It ensures people of all kinds to be able reap the same kinds of benifits that AI technology offers.

Our Mission

As Artificial Intelegence emerges as one of the biggest focal points of modern computing, its flaws are also being examined. People enabled computers to think, but we never taught it to think the right way. So many AI driven technologies are biased towards those who can't voice as loud as the others, and people are finally noticing it. At fAIr, we believe that every individual deserves to be treated equal in the digital world. We are willing to pass down this message and take the first steps towards achieving this goal. Are you?
